Getting there

Broadoak Country House

Bridge Lane, Troutbeck, Windermere

Cumbria, LA23 1LA

How to get there

Whether you drive drive, or get public transport, there will be easy options to get there on the day.

If you are driving, you can use the post code LA23 1LA, and this will take you straight there. It is sign posted as you get close.

There is a train station in Windermere, so if you want to avoid driving, this is an ideal option. From here, it will be a short taxi drive to the venue.

Where to stay

With limited capacity, we can sadly not offer a room at the venue to all guests on the day. However, there are plenty of places to stay local to the venue.

Once you're there

Why not visit some of the local towns and villages

Windermere lake cruise

Whilst in Windermere, why not take a lake cruise to see the beautiful mountains that surround it. Failing that, you can take this up to Ambleside to explore more of what the lakes can offer.

Food & drink

Why not stop and have some food and a pint at one of the local pubs. Bowness-on-Windermere has a range of pubs, most of which being family and dog friendly.

Walking through the hills

If you're up for a walk, then why not go exploring through the beautiful lake district countryside. There are various walks you can do, from easy to a tricky scramble.
