Once you're there

Why not visit some of

the local towns and villages

Windermere lake cruise

Whilst in Windermere, why not take a lake cruise to see the beautiful mountains that surround it. Failing that, you can take this up to Ambleside to explore more of what the lakes can offer

The flying pig

Why not stop and have some food and a pint at one of the local pubs. The flying pig has great food and drink, with the added bonus of being dog friendly

Walking through the holls

If you're up for a walk, then why not go exploring through the beautiful lake district countryside. There are various walks you can do, from easy to a tricky scramble
