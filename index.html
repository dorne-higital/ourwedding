Where to stay?

Windermere

A 5 - 10 minute drive away, this is ideal for staying over. By the lake, there are plenty of bars and restaurants, as well as small shops. There is a train station situated here, so ideal if you do not want to make the drive.

10 - 15 minutes drive, this is a little further out, but just as nice to stay. This will be a litle quieter compared to Windermere, but plenty to do with easy access in and out.
